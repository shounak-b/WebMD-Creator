## Section Guidelines

### Title & Date
- Format: `# WebMD Health Services | [Page Topic]: Content Brief`
- Date: M.D.YYYY (e.g., 3.6.2026)

### Goal of Content
- Bullet points, italicized
- Include: page objective, target personas, conversion goal

### Page Title
- Format: `{Primary Keyword} | WebMD Health Services`
- Length: 50-60 characters
- Note: If the Primary Keyword + " | WebMD Health Services" exceeds 60 characters, shorten the keyword phrase or use "| WebMD" instead

### Meta Description
- Length: 150-160 characters
- Include Primary Keyword naturally

### URL Structure
- Start with `https://www.webmdhealthservices.com/`
- Lowercase, hyphens for spaces

### Target Word Count
- Single number or range from Step 2

### Headings/Sections
See [Headings Format](#headings-format) below.

### Internal Links
- List each URL with anchor text as sub-bullet
- Use "URL TBD" for pages not yet created

## Headings Format

**Formatting:**
- Prefix: `**H1:**`, `**H2:**`, `**H3:**` (bold)
- Headline text: *italicized*
- Intro text: plain italicized paragraph immediately after H1
- Indent: H2 under H1, H3 under H2

**Intro Text (after H1):**

Write one introductory text block immediately after the H1. Content and length vary by page type:

| Page Type | Length | Focus |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Blog / Pillar page | 50–75 words | Cover the core challenge, the solution approach, and what the article will walk through |
| Landing page / Service page | 15–20 words (1–2 sentences) | Concisely answer the "how" behind the H1 — lead with key differentiators or value props; optionally frame as pain point → solution |
| Category page | 15–20 words (1–2 sentences) | Highlight key differentiators or value props for that category |

Apply brand guidelines to intro text (no prohibited terms).

**Special Sections:**
- Testimonials: `[Include testimonial carousel]`
- FAQs: H2 with H3s as questions

**Example:**
```markdown
**H1:** *Employee Well-Being Programs for Large Employers*

*WebMD Health Services delivers a clinically grounded, five-dimension well-being program that drives behavior change and measurable health outcomes for your workforce.*

  **H2:** *Why fragmented point solutions fall short.*

    **H3:** *Low engagement from disconnected experiences.*

    **H3:** *No single view of population health.*

  **H2:** *What our clients are saying.*

  [Include testimonial carousel]
```

**Rules:**
- 1 H1 per page with Primary Keyword
- All headlines ≤60 characters
- H3s only under H2s
- NO writing instructions (added in Step 8)
