# Step 1: Collect Required Information

Before generating headlines, you MUST verify you have all required information. If any required field is missing, ask the user to provide the missing information.

---

## Required Fields

- **Primary Keyword** — The main keyword/phrase this page targets
- **Secondary Keywords** — Additional keywords (comma-separated list)
- **Client Name** — Name of the client/brand (default: **WebMD Health Services**)
- **Page Name** — Internal name for this page
- **Page Type** — Type of page being created (e.g., landing page, blog, pillar page, service page, category page)
- **ICP Information** — Ideal Customer Profile details for this page: target role, market segment, pain points, and goals. See the WebMD Health Services ICP reference below.
- **Competitor Domains** — Competitor domain(s) to analyze (comma-separated if multiple)

## ICP Quick Reference by Segment

### Enterprise (most common — 5,000+ employees)
- **Target roles:** HR Directors, Benefits Coordinators, CHROs, Well-Being Program Administrators, Chief People Officers, Chief Wellness Officers
- **Industries:** Technology, Manufacturing, Financial Services, Health Systems
- **Pain points:** Low employee engagement in well-being programs; difficulty measuring ROI across a geographically diverse workforce; cost pressures; fragmented point solutions that lack cohesion; need for scalable, culturally relevant programming
- **Key needs:** Customizability, scalability, strategic guidance, long-term account support
- **Goals:** Foster a culture of well-being globally; reduce healthcare costs; increase employee productivity and retention; drive measurable behavior change
- **Sphere of influence:** Consultants (Aon, Mercer, WTW, Gallagher, Lockton) are usually involved

### Mid-Market (1,000–5,000 employees)
- **Target roles:** Same as Enterprise, but C-suite is more accessible
- **Pain points:** Very cost-conscious; already have a point solution but want a comprehensive one-stop shop
- **Cares about:** Platform engagement/registration/utilization, wellness challenges, coaching, weight loss programs, straightforward UX
- **Does not prioritize:** ROI at the same level as Enterprise

### Public Sector / Education
- **Target roles:** Well-being Director of HR, dedicated wellness advocate, Board approval required
- **Pain points:** Low utilization, engagement, need for custom reporting; strict procurement timelines (~2 years)
- **Key need:** ROI justification to boards/taxpayers; client stories without confidentiality concerns
- **Competitors in this space:** Personify Health, Sharecare

### Health Plans (National, Blues, Regional)
- **Target roles:** Procurement, Sales/Account Team, Innovation Officers, IT Department
- **Goals:** Attract and retain employer clients; control healthcare costs; promote preventive care; increase member engagement
- **Pain points:** Integrating new well-being programs with existing healthcare systems; proving ROI; addressing social determinants of health

## Optional Fields

- **Existing Page URL** — URL if updating an existing page (helps understand current approach)
- **Page Context / Additional Page Info** — Any extra details about the page, its purpose, or specific requirements

---

## How to Collect Missing Information

1. Review the user's initial message carefully for any provided information
2. If ANY required field is missing, ask the user to provide the specific missing fields
3. Do NOT proceed to Step 2 until all required fields are collected
4. Frame questions clearly and professionally
